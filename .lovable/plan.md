

# PhysioWithUs — Landing Page Plan

## Overview
A modern, conversion-focused landing page for a physiotherapy clinic. Clean healthcare aesthetic with soft blues, whites, and light greens. Mobile-first, designed to generate bookings, calls, and WhatsApp inquiries.

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Sticky Header
- Clinic logo + name on the left
- Navigation links: Services, About, Testimonials, Contact
- "Book Appointment" CTA button (always visible)
- Collapses to hamburger menu on mobile

### 2. Hero Section
- **Headline:** "Expert Physiotherapy Care for Pain-Free Living"
- **Subheadline:** "Personalized treatment plans to help you recover faster and live better."
- Two CTA buttons: **"Book Appointment"** (primary) and **"Call Now"** (secondary)
- Calming background with a subtle gradient overlay
- Trust badges below (e.g., "500+ Patients Treated", "4.9★ Rating", "10+ Years Experience")

### 3. Services Grid
- 6 services in a responsive card grid (3 columns desktop, 2 tablet, 1 mobile)
- Each card: icon, title, short 2-line description
- Services: Sports Injury Rehab, Back & Neck Pain, Post-Surgery Rehab, Pediatric Physiotherapy, Home Visits, Dry Needling / Manual Therapy

### 4. Why Choose Us
- Icon-based feature strip with 5 trust indicators
- Certified Therapists, Personalized Plans, Modern Equipment, Affordable Pricing, Patient-Centered Care
- Clean horizontal layout on desktop, vertical on mobile

### 5. About the Clinic
- Split layout: text on one side, placeholder for doctor/founder photo on the other
- Brief clinic philosophy, years of experience, founder bio
- Warm, approachable tone

### 6. Testimonials
- 4 patient review cards in a carousel/grid
- Star ratings, patient name, condition treated, recovery quote
- Clean card design with subtle shadows

### 7. Appointment Booking Form
- Simple form: Name, Phone, Condition (dropdown), Preferred Date (date picker)
- "Schedule Consultation" CTA button
- Trust note: "We respond within 1 hour"
- Soft background to visually distinguish the section

### 8. Location & Contact
- Clinic address, phone number, timings displayed clearly
- Google Maps embed placeholder
- WhatsApp contact button

### 9. Footer
- Quick links, services list, contact info, social media icons
- Privacy Policy link
- Copyright notice

### 10. Floating Elements
- **WhatsApp floating button** — bottom-right corner, always visible
- **Mobile sticky bottom bar** — three buttons: Call | Book | WhatsApp (visible only on mobile)

---

## Design & UX
- **Color palette:** Soft blue (#3B82F6 range), white backgrounds, light green accents for trust elements
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, large readable text, proper heading hierarchy (H1→H3)
- **Mobile-first:** All sections optimized for touch, large tap targets, minimal text blocks
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll-triggered fade-ins for sections
- **No backend needed** — form will show a success toast (can be connected to a backend later)
